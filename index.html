# My Work

In 2020 I joined The Graph as a protocol engineer, contributing to the largest blockchain indexing protocol. We built and launched the Graph Network with a small team, and after a few years, I had the opportunity to lead the entire engineering team.

In 2017, I became a founding engineer at Decentraland, where I led a team in developing the first-ever community-owned Metaverse. Our team pioneered a vast virtual world with an in-game economy, empowering players with ownership of digital assets.

Before that, in 2016, I served as CTO of PopChest, where we built a video streaming platform that allowed fans to directly pay content creators without intermediaries. That project led me to San Mateo, California, where I joined Tribe 8 of Boost VC incubator.
